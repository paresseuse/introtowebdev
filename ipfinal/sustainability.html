Alternative Fabrics

The fashion industry uses vast amounts of water, energy and chemicals like fertilizers and dyes in its production processes

Additionally, the ever-popular polyester-type materials add to ocean plastic pollution since they contain microplastics.

SOLUTIONS: Support environmentally conscious labels such as Reformation, Alternative Apparel, and H&M Conscious that utilize recycled fabrics and alternative fabrics using zero-damage dyes. Additionally, donate your scrap fabrics to organizations like FabScrap that recycle your fabrics for you!
