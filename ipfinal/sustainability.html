Alternative Fabrics

PROBLEM:

The fashion industry uses vast amounts of water, energy and chemicals like fertilizers and dyes in its production processes

Additionally, the ever-popular polyester-type materials add to ocean plastic pollution since they contain microplastics.



HOVER FOR SOLUTIONS!

Alternative Fabrics

SOLUTIONS:

Look into washing machine filters that aid in preventing microplastic release such as the “Guppy Friend” washing bag by Langbrett

Wear only natural fabrics—cotton, wool, hemp—that will eventually break down in the environment

Consider switching to a liquid laundry soap—laundry powder detergents allow a bigger release of microfibers

Use a colder wash setting and wash for a shorter cycle—Higher temperature can damage clothes and release more fibers
