PROBLEM:



Deadstock is a huge problem in the fashion industry for factories creating excess stock that usually gets destroyed afterwards, resulting in tons of fabric waste, even burning of stock as seen with Burberry.

Preloved & Reworked

SOLUTIONS:

Support a circular economy which encourages the circulation of pre-loved goods to ensure full usage of an item.

Look into reselling platform apps such as Depop, Poshmark and Mercari for preworn, vintage or reworked clothes

Always check out your local vintage/consignment stores for unique items as well!

Support upcycled fashion in contemporary brands such as the Urban Renewal label at Urban Outfitters, ASOS Reclaimed Vintage, and Nike Grind
