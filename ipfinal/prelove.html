Preloved and Reworked

Deadstock is a huge problem in the fashion industry for factories creating excess stock that usually gets destroyed afterwards, resulting in tons of fabric waste, even burning of stock as seen with Burberry.

SOLUTIONS: We can support a circular economy which encourages the circulation of pre-loved goods to ensure full usage of an item. There are many apps such as Depop and Poshmark which help users buy and sell preworn, vintage or reworked clothes and you can always check out your local vintage/consignment stores for unique items. Additionally, many shops are selling reworked items such as the Urban Renewal label at Urban Outfitters, ASOS Reclaimed Vintage, and Nike Grind.
